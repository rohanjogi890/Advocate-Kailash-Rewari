01 / Profile

A Decade at the Bar.
A Lifetime of Service.

Advocate Kailash Chand was enrolled with the Bar Council under registration number P/3873/2016 and has been engaged in active legal practice from the courts of Rewari, Haryana since 2016.

Over a decade of professional engagement, his work has spanned the spectrum of criminal trial, civil litigation, family disputes, property matters, cheque dishonour proceedings, labour adjudication and arbitration.

Alongside professional practice, he maintains a sustained commitment to community-oriented social work, including legal awareness activities and assistance to under-served sections in navigating administrative processes.
